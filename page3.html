Token Vesting Period

For the first week post-lunch, only 70% of the tokens will be claimed the remaining 30% will be vested for a period of one week. This vesting period is crucial to stabilize the market and prevent large, sudden sell-offs. after this week, investors a can claim at the REMANING 30% of their tokens. this approach ensure a smoother and more stable market entry for koiner.

Stability Tax

A temporary Stability Tax of 20% will be applied to all transactions for the first 48 hours after the launch. This measure is designed to deter rapid trading and speculative behaviour that can lead to market volatility. After the initial 48-hour period, this tax rate will drop to zero, making the platform more accessible for investors and more appealing to centralized exchanges.
